Selected work

Four campaigns, four stories

Each case study follows the same structure: the brief, the strategic decision made, and the measurable outcome.

01
Healthcare · Meta Ads
Dental Promo — RGH's most efficient campaign ever
Radiant Group of Hospitals
Outcome
KES 11.22 CPR
3,136 conversations · KES 35,193 spend
The brief
Drive patient inquiries for dental services across multiple RGH branches, reaching both existing patients and new audiences in Nairobi at a sustainable cost per conversation.
Strategic approach
Developed a promotional offer framing that lowered perceived barriers to dental visits — a service category with historically low spontaneous demand. Used Meta's messaging-optimized objective targeting warm audiences (previous page engagers) while running a parallel cold audience campaign with a broader interest pool. Ad creative combined a clear price anchor with reassuring clinical imagery. Audience segmentation was refined mid-flight based on early engagement signals, reallocating budget to the top-performing ad set.
Results
Best CPR in all RGH history — KES 11.22 3,136 patient conversations KES 35,193 total spend Benchmark for all future dental campaigns
02
Education · Enrollment
CNA Intake — record-low cost student acquisition
Radiant Hospital Training Institute
Outcome
KES 7.77 CPR
3,318 conversations · 1,221 new contacts
The brief
Fill intake seats for the Certified Nursing Assistant (CNA) programme at RHTI, reaching prospective students across Nairobi and key upcountry towns at the lowest possible cost per lead.
Strategic approach
Shifted the campaign objective from standard awareness to messaging-optimized engagement — prioritizing conversations over link clicks — based on the insight that students needed to ask questions before committing to enrollment. Creative leaned into the career transformation narrative: clear graduate employment pathways and NHIF accreditation as key proof points. Geographic targeting was split into Nairobi-dense and countrywide layers with separate budget allocation, enabling performance comparison by region. Carousel format was deployed to showcase multiple programme benefits in a single unit.
Results
Lowest CPR in RHTI account history 3,318 student conversations 1,221 new contacts added Strategy replicated across 3 subsequent intakes
03
Product Launch · Healthcare Access
Radiant Afyaplan (RAP) Card — end-to-end product rollout
Radiant Group of Hospitals
Scope
Concept → Stakeholder Alignment → Full Launch
The brief
Design and launch a healthcare access product — a prepaid care card — that would expand RGH's patient base beyond NHIF-covered populations, improving affordability and increasing service uptake across branches.
Strategic approach
Led the project from initial concept through full implementation: conducted stakeholder research to define product tiers and benefit structures, drafted the product communication framework, developed brand identity and collateral for the card, coordinated alignment sessions with clinical teams and branch managers, and designed the patient-facing enrollment and activation process. Produced all internal training materials to ensure consistent product knowledge across 5 branches.
Results
Full product lifecycle managed solo Cross-functional alignment across 5 branches Complete brand identity & collateral developed Patient-facing and internal comms delivered
04
Publishing · Digital Turnaround
Management Magazine — commercial and digital revival
Kenya Institute of Management
Key Outcomes
+150%
Social engagement · 85% web traffic growth · 30% ad revenue increase
The brief
Reverse the declining readership and advertising revenue of KIM's flagship Management Magazine, repositioning it as a relevant, digitally-led business publication for Kenya's management professionals.
Strategic approach
Implemented a digital-first editorial strategy: restructured content production workflows to prioritize SEO-optimized long-form articles, built a digital distribution calendar, and introduced data-driven audience segmentation for targeted circulation. Standardized editorial guidelines across all branches to enforce 100% brand consistency. Developed a B2B partnership pitch for advertising, targeting aligned brands with audience match data. Grew subscriber base by over 1,200 readers through strategic distribution partnerships and managed 20+ media, alumni, and corporate sponsor relationships.
Results
150% social media engagement growth 85% increase in web traffic 30% advertising revenue increase 1,200+ new subscribers acquired
